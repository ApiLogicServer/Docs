## Declarative GenAI  
### Summary
GenAI-Logic transforms natural language into enterprise-grade systems.

NL describes *what must be true* ‚Äî not how to compute it.  
GenAI-Logic captures that intent, generates deterministic rules (DL) and probabilistic handlers (PL), and executes them with a governed rules engine.

Declarative logic removes procedural glue-code, reduces complexity,  
and avoids ‚ÄúFranken-responses‚Äù that hide logic across controller code.

This is the beginning of a **Business Logic Agent** ‚Äî logic you can read, trust, and evolve.
